Problems We Solved

Main pain point

01

Pricing decisions are intuitive and suboptimal

Sales representatives rely on intuition or guesswork, without visibility into:

  • Historical similar deals and how are they priced
  • Customer willingness to pay, company margin targets, cost drivers, or discount programs

Consequence:

  • Manual and slow pricing process
  • Over-discounts and 5% - 25% margin leakage
  • Pricing inconsistencies for similar deal contexts

Other pain points

02

Experience-based configuration is incompatible and costly

Sales reps give low-quality configurations:

  • Receive vague customer requests
  • Guess the right product model from thousands of SKUs
  • Misapply bundling and discounts

Consequence:

  • Lost potential deals
  • Margin lost due to costly procurement
  • Missed upselling/cross-selling opportunities
03

Manual quoting is costly, slow, and error-prone

Generating a quote takes hours due to:

  • Manual copy-pasting
  • Constant revision
  • Lack of consistent standards

Consequence:

  • Manual errors
  • Long customer waiting time
  • Low customer satisfaction
04

Manual updating CRM / ERP, billing & logistics is time-consuming

Frequent manual CRM / ERP, billing & logistics updates after each quote lead to:

  • Repetitive, low-value updates drive careless entries
  • Low ROI on time that could be better spent on high-value selling activities

Consequence:

  • Poor CRM / ERP, billing & logistics data quality
  • Undermined analytics, forecasting, and resource planning
