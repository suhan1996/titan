Integration

We already have a CPQ tool and don't want a big replacement project—can Titan AI still help?

Yes. While Titan AI's end-to-end CPQ platform offers the fastest automation and smoothest user experience, you can also use Titan AI as a plug-in with your current CPQ system

Drop-in integration

Titan AI reads and writes directly to your existing CPQ, injecting automated configuration and pricing suggestions right into your team's familiar screens.

Modular activation

Only turn on the features you want, such as pricing intelligence, quote automation, or cross-sell prompts—while keeping your current UI and workflows.

Pricing

What's the pricing model—how much should I expect to invest?

We use an innovative token-based pricing model designed for flexibility and fairness.

Low upfront cost

Only a cost-covering, one-time fee for development and integration, including setup, customization, and connecting to your systems.

Usage-based charging

After setup, fees are charged based on usage activity, with tokens serving as the standard unit. Smaller quotes consume fewer tokens, while larger or more complex tasks require more.

No usage, no charge

When the agent is inactive, no need to pay anything, simple as that
